Experience a night of timeless artistry with violin sensation Aubree Oliverson and Conductor Christopher Dragon. The program opens with Rossini's playful Barber of Seville Overture before Oliverson dazzles in Mendelssohn's beloved Violin Concerto. The evening concludes with Beethoven's iconic Symphony No. 5, a masterpiece of fate and triumph.
