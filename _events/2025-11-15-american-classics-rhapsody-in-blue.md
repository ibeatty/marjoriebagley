Celebrate the spirit of American music with the return of pianist Gabriela Martinez and Conductor Christopher Dragon. Enjoy Florence Price's powerful Concert Overture No. 2, Gershwin's jazzy Rhapsody in Blue, Bernstein's vibrant West Side Story Overture, and Copland's energetic music from the complete ballet Rodeo.
