Experience the monumental power of Verdi's Requiem performed by the Greensboro Symphony Orchestra with Music Director Christopher Dragon, guest soloists Danielle Talamantes (soprano), Stephanie Foley Davis (mezzo soprano), John Pickle (tenor), and Richard Ollarsaba (bass baritone), joined by the Winston Salem Symphony Chorus. A monumental work of drama and emotion, it's a performance not to be missed.
