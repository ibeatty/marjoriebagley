Studying with Marjorie

Marjorie teaches violinists in UNCG's BM, MM, and DMA programs through weekly one-on-one lessons, and coaches student chamber groups. She remains fascinated by the unique qualities of each student, combining violin technique and learning strategies to help them move toward their potential.

Biography

First violinist and founding member of the Arcata String Quartet, with concerts from Wigmore Hall to Weill Recital Hall; three decades of performances across five continents; recordings on the Equilibrium, Centaur, Albany, Summit, and VOX labels.
