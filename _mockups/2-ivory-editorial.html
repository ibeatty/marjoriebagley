Professor of Violin at UNC Greensboro, Concertmaster of the Greensboro Symphony, and a chamber musician whose collaborations have reached five continents.

Three decades of performance, from London's Wigmore Hall to Carnegie's Weill Recital Hall — and a teaching studio devoted to helping each violinist move toward their own potential.
