02 · StudioStudying with Marjorie

Violin study at UNC Greensboro in the BM, MM, and DMA programs: weekly one-on-one lessons and chamber coaching. Marjorie remains fascinated by the unique qualities of each student, combining violin technique and learning strategies to help them move toward their potential.

03 · StoryA life in performance

Founding first violinist of the Arcata String Quartet — Wigmore Hall, Weill Recital Hall, a decade of touring — with recordings on the Equilibrium, Centaur, Albany, Summit, and VOX labels, and performances on five continents.
