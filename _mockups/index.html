Five design directions

Mockups for the marjoriebagley.com redesign — same content in each, so the comparison is purely visual. Open each; imagine living with it for five years.

  1. Concert Program

    Burgundy on warm ivory with hairline gold rules; set like the title page of a printed program. The maroon-family direction — continuity with the current site.

  2. Ivory Editorial

    Near-monochrome literary elegance: small-caps masthead, Crimson body, one oxblood accent. The quietest direction; events as a restrained text list.

  3. Conservatory Modern

    The one sans-serif direction, steel blue and white, with the portrait photo and three role cards (Studio / Orchestra / Chamber). Closest to how peer institutions present faculty.

  4. Engraved Edition

    Garamond set like a Henle/Bärenreiter score title page, staff-line ornaments, dotted-leader season index. The most austere and most "classical music" of the five.

  5. Piedmont Dusk

    Deep spruce-green bands with warm cream and a terracotta accent; first-person introduction as the hero. The most distinctive palette while staying sober.

Notes for review. Event listings show real 2026–27 GSO season titles, but which concerts (and any solo/recital entries, e.g. "Faculty Recital") are illustrative — no claim about what Marjorie is actually playing. All five follow the anti-staleness rules from the design research: no photo dependence (except the portrait in #3), no empty-able widgets, season framing, ≤5 nav items. Elements can be mixed — e.g. #4's staff lines in #1's palette, or #5's first-person hero in #2's typography.
