The Greensboro Symphony Orchestra presented cellist Bryan Cheng with Principal Guest Conductor Chelsea Tipton in a program featuring Coleridge-Taylor's Danse Negre, Elgar's Cello Concerto, and Brahms' Symphony No. 3.

See [the GSO's event page](https://greensborosymphony.org/event/elgars-cello-concerto/) for details.
