The Greensboro Symphony Orchestra featured guitarists Thomas Flippin and D. J. Sparr with Guest Conductor Robert Moody in a program including Montgomery's Strum, Brubeck's Double Guitar Concerto, and Tchaikovsky's Symphony No. 5.

See [the GSO's event page](https://greensborosymphony.org/event/brubecks-double-guitar-concerto/) for details.
