title: "GSO Masterworks: Mozart's Requiem"
---

The Greensboro Symphony Orchestra performed Mozart's sacred masterworks with soprano Maria Valdes and the GSO Master Chorale, led by Principal Guest Conductor Chelsea Tipton. The program featured Mozart's Ave verum corpus, Exultate, jubilate, and Requiem.

See [the GSO's event page](https://greensborosymphony.org/event/mozart-requiem/) for details.
