title: "GSO Masterworks: We've Got Rhythm!"
---

Immerse yourself in Glory and Grandeur with Greensboro Symphony percussionists Wiley Sykes, John Beck, and Eric Willie, led by Principal Guest Conductor Chelsea Tipton. The program includes Mason's lively Toast of the Town, Debussy's dreamy Prelude to the Afternoon of a Faun, Peck's dynamic Glory and Grandeur, and the majestic Dvorak Symphony No. 7.

See [the GSO's event page](https://greensborosymphony.org/event/weve-got-rhythm/) for details.
