Join us for the closing Masterworks concert of the season with an evening of orchestral splendor featuring harpist Courtney Hershey Bress and Music Director Christopher Dragon. The program includes Berlioz's spirited Hungarian March, Michael Daugherty's captivating Harp of the Age, and Mussorgsky's iconic Pictures at an Exhibition.

See [the GSO's event page](https://greensborosymphony.org/event/orchestral-fireworks/) for details.
