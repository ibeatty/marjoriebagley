Marjorie Bagley is a Professor of Violin in the [School of Music](https://vpa.uncg.edu/music/) at the University of North Carolina Greensboro (UNCG), and Concertmaster of the [Greensboro Symphony Orchestra](https://greensborosymphony.org/). She also performs solo and in chamber ensembles with a diverse range of colleagues in North Carolina and beyond.
