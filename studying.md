Marjorie is Professor of Violin with the [School of Music](https://vpa.uncg.edu/music/) in UNCG's College of Visual and Performing Arts. She teaches violin students in the Bachelor of Music in Performance, Bachelor of Music in Music Education, Master of Music in Performance, and Doctor of Musical Arts degrees through weekly one-on-one lessons. She also coaches student chamber groups.

- For information about the degree programs, see [College of Visual and Performing Arts ≫ Music ≫ Music Degrees and Programs](https://vpa.uncg.edu/music/degrees-and-programs/).
- For information about applying, see [College of Visual and Performing Arts ≫ Music ≫ Apply](https://vpa.uncg.edu/music/apply/).

Marjorie does not generally teach private students, though she does occasionally make an exception for a high-school student of exceptional promise and dedication. <span style="color: red;">(Is this something you want to address at all?)</span>

She has no near-term plans to teach at summer music festivals.
